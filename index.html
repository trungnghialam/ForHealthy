IT Technologies

IT Technologies

In this section we are going to talk about the following IT technologies.

Clouds, services, servers
Cloud computing is a paradigm that allows on-demand network access to shared computing resources. The state of art of this technology is its 3 models SaaS (Software as a Service), PaaS (Platform as a Service) and IaaS (Infrastructure as a Service).

Autonomous Vehicles can also be known as driverless cars or you can simply say cars on auto pilot. This is one of the most emerging technologies these days. The cars are definitely getting smarter and the concept of autonomous vehicles is undoubtedly getting better and better with the progress the different companies have shown but the day we will be able to buy one of these cars or even ride in one is a lot further out then it may seem.

Blockchains and Crytocurrency: A blockchain is a distributed ledger that is completely open to anyone, they have interesting property: once some data has been recorded inside a blockchain, it becomes very difficult to change. Each block contains some data the hash of the block and the hash of the previous block. The data that is stored inside the block depends on the type of block chain; For Example the bitcoin blockchain consist of details about a transaction such as sender receiver and the amount of coins.

Natural Language Processing and Chatterbots: Natural Language Processing or NLP is an area of computer science that involves processing (understanding and synthesizing) of spoken and/or written language. It is meant to enable interactions between human and machines in humans in a ‘language’ that is more natural for humans.
